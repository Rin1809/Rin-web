# The Ultimate Guide to Kitten Naps

Napping is not just a hobby for kittens; it's a lifestyle. In this guide, we'll explore the science behind why kittens sleep so much and how to ensure they get the best rest possible.

![Sleepy Kitten](/cat-blog-1.png)

## Why Do Kittens Sleep So Much?
Kittens are bundles of energy when they are awake, but growing requires a lot of sleep. A hormone called growth hormone is only released when they are sleeping, so napping is essential for their development.
